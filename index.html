FuelTrack is a personal fuel and mileage tracker for iPhone and iPad. Log every fill-up and let FuelTrack calculate your real MPG, cost per mile, and estimated range — no OBD-II dongle, no accounts, no subscriptions.

Common questions

Why is my MPG empty? Real MPG needs at least two full-tank fill-ups to calculate. Log one more complete fill and you'll see it appear.

Can I export my data? Not in v1.0 — export is planned for 1.1. Your data lives in the app container and is included in encrypted iCloud device backups if you have those enabled.

Does FuelTrack work without location? Yes — you can log fill-ups and maintenance without granting location. GPS is only needed if you want automatic drive tracking (distance, speed, top speed).
