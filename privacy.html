Privacy Policy

FuelTrack for iOS · Effective July 6, 2026

FuelTrack is a personal fuel and mileage tracker. This policy explains what information the app handles and — importantly — what it does not.

Short version

FuelTrack does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. Everything you enter — vehicles, fill-ups, trips, and maintenance records — is stored only on your iPhone or iPad.

Data we do not collect

  • No account is required — there is no sign-up, sign-in, or user profile.
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are embedded in the app.
  • No usage telemetry is sent to us or to any third party.
  • No email address, phone number, name, or other contact information is requested or transmitted.

Data stored on your device

The following information is written to local storage in your app's private container so that FuelTrack can display it back to you across launches:

  • Vehicle details you enter (name, make, model, year, odometer, tank size).
  • Fuel fill-ups you log (date, gallons, price, station name, odometer).
  • Trips recorded on your device (start and end times, distance, average and top speed, optional start/end location labels).
  • Maintenance items you set up (name, recurrence, notes, due dates).

This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling FuelTrack removes it permanently.

Location data

FuelTrack uses your device's GPS only while you are actively recording a drive from within the app. Location samples are used to compute distance and speed for the current trip and are stored on your device as part of that trip record. Location data is never sent to a server and never shared with any third party. You can revoke location access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → FuelTrack.

Third-party services

FuelTrack does not integrate with any third-party services, ad networks, analytics providers, or cloud sync backends in this version.

Children's privacy

FuelTrack is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of FuelTrack changes how data is handled (for example, optional cloud sync), this policy will be updated and the effective date at the top of this page will change. Continued use of the app after such changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
